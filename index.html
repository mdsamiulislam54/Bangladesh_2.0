"Each of us has enormous power to make an impact. We can create a world where poverty does not exist, where every person, no matter how poor, has access to the tools and opportunities to unleash their potential. Poverty is not a reflection of individual failure but a symptom of broken systems that fail to recognize and nurture the inherent creativity and capability of people. We need to redesign those systems, focusing not on profit alone, but on solving human problems, ensuring that wealth creation serves humanity rather than just a select few. If we all contribute, even in small ways, together, we can create a world that is fair, just, and inclusive."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
